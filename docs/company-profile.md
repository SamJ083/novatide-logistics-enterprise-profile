# Company Profile

## Executive Summary

NovaTide Logistics is a fictional multinational logistics and supply chain organization used as the canonical enterprise case study for the **NovaTide Enterprise Simulation**.

The company provides integrated logistics services spanning maritime shipping, port operations, inland transportation, warehousing, customs brokerage, and digital supply chain solutions. Operating across North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, the Middle East, and Latin America, NovaTide serves customers in highly regulated industries including manufacturing, healthcare, retail, energy, and government.

Over the past several years, NovaTide has invested heavily in digital transformation, cloud adoption, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence to improve operational resilience, supply chain visibility, and customer experience.

This repository establishes the baseline enterprise context used across all Governance Series projects, including cybersecurity governance, third-party risk management (TPRM), AI governance, regulatory compliance, enterprise risk management, and business continuity.

NovaTide Logistics delivers end-to-end supply chain services combining physical transportation with digital logistics capabilities.

Core services include:

- Maritime freight transportation
- Port and terminal logistics
- Customs brokerage and trade compliance
- Warehousing and distribution
- Intermodal transportation
- Supply chain visibility
- Fleet optimization
- Cold-chain logistics
- Last-mile delivery coordination
- Digital logistics platforms

To support these services, NovaTide continues to modernize its operations through cloud-native platforms, IoT-enabled assets, predictive analytics, and responsible adoption of artificial intelligence.
