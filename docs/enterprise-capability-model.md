# Enterprise Capability Model

## Purpose

The Enterprise Capability Model defines the core business capabilities that enable NovaTide Logistics to deliver value to customers.

Unlike organizational structures, which may change over time, business capabilities represent **what the organization must be able to do**, regardless of how teams are organized.

This model provides the foundation for governance, enterprise architecture, cybersecurity, AI governance, third-party risk management, and business continuity planning.

---

# Capability Hierarchy

NovaTide's capabilities are organized into three layers:

- Strategic Capabilities
- Operational Capabilities
- Enterprise Support Capabilities

---

# Strategic Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Supply Chain Management | End-to-end planning and execution of global logistics operations. |
| Customer Experience | Digital services and customer engagement across the shipment lifecycle. |
| Digital Innovation | Development and deployment of technology-enabled logistics solutions. |
| Risk & Compliance | Enterprise governance, regulatory compliance, and operational resilience. |

# Capability Ownership

Each capability has:

- Executive Sponsor
- Business Owner
- Technology Owner
- Risk Owner

Ownership ensures accountability for operational performance, resilience, cybersecurity, and regulatory compliance.
