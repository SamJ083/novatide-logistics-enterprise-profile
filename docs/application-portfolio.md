# Enterprise Application Portfolio

## Overview

NovaTide Logistics relies on a diverse portfolio of enterprise applications to support global logistics operations, corporate services, customer engagement, cybersecurity, and data-driven decision making.

This document provides a high-level inventory of the organization's strategic business applications and establishes the baseline for future governance, cybersecurity, AI governance, and third-party risk management projects.

---

# Application Classification

Applications are classified into four categories based on business criticality.

| Classification | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Critical | Required to maintain core logistics operations or regulatory obligations. |
| High | Supports major business functions with limited short-term workarounds. |
| Medium | Supports internal productivity or departmental operations. |
| Low | Convenience applications with minimal operational impact. |

# Application Ownership

Each application has an assigned business owner responsible for operational performance and a technology owner responsible for availability, security, and lifecycle management.

Ownership is maintained within the enterprise configuration management process.

---

# Integration Principles

Enterprise applications exchange information through secured APIs and managed integration services.

Integration objectives include:

- Data integrity
- Secure authentication
- High availability
- Regulatory compliance
- Auditability
- Operational resilience

---

# AI-Enabled Applications

Several applications include embedded artificial intelligence capabilities.

Examples include:

- Azure OpenAI Service
- NaviEdge Edge Platform
- NovaTrack
- FleetPulse
- Power BI (Copilot)
- Microsoft 365 Copilot

These systems are subject to additional governance requirements under NovaTide's AI Governance Program.

---

# Governance

Applications are reviewed throughout their lifecycle for:

- Business value
- Security posture
- Vendor performance
- Regulatory compliance
- Operational resilience
- Third-party dependencies

Critical applications receive enhanced monitoring and are prioritized within business continuity, disaster recovery, and third-party risk management activities.
