# Business Model

## Overview

NovaTide Logistics operates an integrated global logistics ecosystem that combines physical transportation, digital supply chain platforms, and data-driven services.

The company delivers end-to-end logistics solutions across the entire supply chain lifecycle - from shipment planning and customs processing to warehousing, transportation, and final delivery.

Rather than operating as a traditional shipping company, NovaTide positions itself as a technology-enabled logistics partner, using cloud platforms, AI, IoT, and analytics to improve visibility, operational efficiency, and customer experience.

---

# Value Proposition

NovaTide creates value by enabling customers to:

- Move goods efficiently across global supply chains.
- Improve shipment visibility through real-time tracking.
- Reduce operational risk with resilient logistics services.
- Accelerate customs and regulatory compliance.
- Optimize transportation costs.
- Improve sustainability through data-driven logistics planning.
- Make informed decisions using predictive analytics and AI-assisted insights.

# Core Business Capabilities

The organization operates through eight core business capabilities.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| Freight Management | Ocean, air, rail, and road transportation planning and execution. |

| Warehousing & Distribution | Inventory management, storage, fulfillment, and cross-docking. |

# Operating Model

NovaTide follows a product-centric operating model supported by shared enterprise services.

Business teams own customer outcomes, while centralized technology, cybersecurity, risk, and compliance functions provide governance and operational support.

Cross-functional teams include:

- Business Operations
- Product Management
- Software Engineering
- Cloud Engineering
- Cybersecurity
- Data Science
- AI Engineering
- Enterprise Architecture
- Governance, Risk & Compliance
- Legal & Compliance
